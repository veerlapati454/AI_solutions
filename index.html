FAQ

Common questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about Signal.

What data sources does Signal support?
We support over 60 integrations including Snowflake, BigQuery, AWS S3, PostgreSQL, and major SaaS tools like Slack, Salesforce, and Stripe.
How long does setup take?
Most teams are up and running in under 15 minutes. Connect your first data source, and Signal starts detecting patterns immediately.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Signal is SOC 2 Type II certified with encryption at rest and in transit. We also offer SSO and role-based access control.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. No long-term contracts. You can cancel or change your plan at any time from your account settings.
