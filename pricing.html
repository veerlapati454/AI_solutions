Quick answers to common questions about our pricing and plans.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to get started.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and for Enterprise plans, we offer invoice-based billing.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel anytime with no penalties or hidden fees. Your data will remain accessible for 30 days.
